Chapter 1 – What is Democracy? Why Democracy?

What is Democracy?

Democracy is a form of government where the rulers are elected by the people. It is based on the principle of political equality.

Key Features of Democracy

  • Major decisions by elected leaders
  • Free and fair elections
  • Each adult has one vote and one value
  • Rule of law and respect for rights

Arguments Against Democracy

  • Leaders keep changing, leading to instability
  • Political competition often leads to corruption
  • Ordinary people do not know what is good for them

Arguments in Favour of Democracy

  • More accountable to the people
  • Improves decision-making quality
  • Provides a method to deal with differences
  • Enhances dignity of citizens
  • Allows correction of mistakes

Broader Meanings of Democracy

It is not just limited to elections and government formation, but also includes the spirit of equality, freedom, and justice in all areas of life.
